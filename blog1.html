Order Flow Dynamics

24th February,2022

Introduction: Without even delving into order flow charts, it is important to understand what’s going on behind the scenes. Charts and price action always tell you a story, the story is not always as simple as the ‘Market Maker wants to rekt everyone.’ Let’s start with a metaphor.

Assume this painting by Picasso was on sale at a famous auction house. At first, there are a lot of buyers for the painting competing amongst themselves, every art enthusiast in the world wants this picture! What would happen? The price would move up. It would move up in an insane manner. Ultimately, it will go to the highest bidder. At the same time, a lot of people would drop out of the bidding race, they would think, “I wanted this but I can’t pay these prices.” Another section of bidders will stay in the running but still think twice, “How much more am I willing to pay?” Simple enough? Now in the context of financial markets, this is how this exact concept plays out in terms of bids for the price of an asset. Auction House: Sellers, Bidders: Buyers.
